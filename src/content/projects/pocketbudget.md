PocketBudget is a personal finance and budget tracker built for Android. It's designed around a single principle: your financial data belongs on your device, not in the cloud.

## What It Does

- Track income and expenses with categories
- View transaction history and spending patterns
- Dashboard with balance overview
- Fully offline — no internet required, no account needed
- Data stored locally with SQLite

## Tech Stack

Built with **React Native + Expo** for cross-platform development, with **SQLite** via Expo SQLite for local-first data persistence. **AdMob** powers the free tier with non-intrusive banner ads.

## Why Local-First

Most budget apps sync to the cloud. That means your spending habits, income, and financial patterns live on someone else's server. PocketBudget takes the opposite approach — everything stays on your device. No signup, no sync, no server.
