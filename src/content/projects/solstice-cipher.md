A browser-based puzzle game built in Godot 4 for the June Solstice Game Jam. Solstice Cipher teaches its optical mechanics through an unobtrusive, context-aware tutorial — dynamic tweens and signals guide players without breaking immersion.

Play it on the web via [GitHub Pages](https://fanioz.github.io/solstice-cipher/) or the [Vercel mirror](https://solsticechiper.vercel.app/). Read the dev journal on [building the tutorial UI](/blog/solstice-chiper).
