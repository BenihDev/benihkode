A free, offline puzzle game for Android, built in Godot 4 for the June Solstice Game Jam. Solstice Cipher teaches its optical mechanics through an unobtrusive, context-aware tutorial — dynamic tweens and signals guide players without breaking immersion.

**Heads up:** the Android app is currently in Google Play **internal testing**, so it isn't publicly downloadable yet. To get access:

1. Join the **[benihkode](https://groups.google.com/g/benihkode)** Google Group.
2. Once you're a member, open the app on [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.benihkode.solsticechiper) with the same Google account to download it.

Prefer to just try it? Play the web build on [GitHub Pages](https://fanioz.github.io/solstice-cipher/) / [Vercel](https://solsticechiper.vercel.app/). Read the dev journal on [building the tutorial UI](/blog/solstice-chiper).
