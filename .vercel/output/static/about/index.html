About

The Story Behind BenihKode

BenihKode means "Seed of Code" — a name that reflects the philosophy that every great application begins as a tiny idea, a seed that needs nurturing, patience, and care to grow into something real and meaningful.

This is a developer's garden — a place where ideas are planted as Product Requirement Documents, nurtured through development, and eventually harvested as shipped products. Along the way, the journey is documented through honest journal entries about the joys, frustrations, and breakthroughs of building software.

What You'll Find Here

  • Product Ideas 💡 — Seeds waiting to be planted. Full PRDs with visions, specs, and dreams.
  • Shipped Projects 🚀 — From seed to harvest. Real projects that made it out of the garden.
  • Dev Journal 📝 — Notes, learnings, and stories from the trenches. Growing in public.

The Philosophy

Not every seed becomes a tree. Some ideas stay as seeds, some start growing but pivot into something else entirely, and some bloom beautifully. The point isn't perfection — it's the act of planting, nurturing, and learning from every attempt.

This garden is built with Astro, powered by Cloudflare, and designed with a love for clean code and thoughtful interfaces.
